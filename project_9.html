The Chick Vacc App

2017 / 2 months / User Research + Product Design

Chick Vacc is an app designed for Merial Animal Health Ltd. to help poultry farmers improve work efficiency, satisfaction, and the vaccination rate of baby chicks. I started with phone interview and went on to spend 2 days in the poultry farm for on-site user observation.

Some of the key findings: 1) Poultry farmers have a hard time typing on a digital device while they're working, so enabling voice input is crucial. 2) Farmers find it tedious and time-consuming to record vaccination data. For that, I simplified the flow with one-click options, color-coded icons and micro-animations to make it intuitive and "fun". The recorded data was presented with vertical scrolling effects to be visually pleasing & informative. 3) Farmers lack the channels to seek professional advice. That often leads to failed vaccination attempts. Thus I designed the Ask an Expert/ Training Videos section and made sure the materials are easy to understand.

As confirmed by follow-up interviews, the Chick Vacc app improved farmers' work efficiency and satisfaction with a 60 NPS(Net Promoter Score). It also brought a 30% increase in trackable chick vaccination rate.
