Mastercard Donation Platform

2019 / 2 months / Product Design + Iteration

Mastercard Donation Platform is a solution for busy young working professionals in their late 20s or early 30s who just want an easy way to donate to their cared causes. The user journey is three-parted. 1) Set up regular auto-donation for one's favourite charities. 2) One-off donations to a special campaign/emergency. 3) A separate Donation Page to track impact and adjust settings.

I led design workshops with product and tech team to clarify the problem + user story + product features. Then I designed the demo (from wireframe to high-fid interactive prototype) which was presented at the Oct 2019’s Global Inclusive Growth Summit and praised by Mastercard CEO Ajay. Late 2019, it was launched for Citi Bank, among other clients.

Key design decisions I made: 1) Combine data with personal narrative to move people 2) Use social pressuring for the good 3) Design "slow experiences". Allow the content to soak into the user and build up emotional ties over time for long-term donation commitments
