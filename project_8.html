The Green Initiative

2019 / 1 week / Rapid Prototyping + High-Fedility Design

Green Initiative is an attempt to encourage environmentally conscious behaviors and make the planet greener. Each Mastercard user will be rewarded with “green points” every time they take public transit, or shop in environmentally-friendly stores, etc. They can then use those green points to buy and plant real trees in designated places, and witness their impact grow over time.

I was responsible for designing the initial in quick turnaround. My design helped the product owners win support from executives and it was subsequently developed into the Mastercard Priceless Planet Coalition (launched Jan, 2020) which promises to plant 100 million trees on the earth in 5 years.

To "nudge" users for a greener life 1) Vivid colors, icons, and leaf themed micro-animations were used to imbue the sense of life and hope. 2) User's impact was compared to the number of vehicle's CO2 emission being reduced per year. They'll also see the planted trees show up in real-time in the 3D world map on the app homepage. 3) Positive social pressuring: share on social media | plant with friends | "only 70 points away from planting your next tree.." | "Your neighbors have been planting more trees.."
