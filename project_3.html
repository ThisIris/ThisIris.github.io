Smart Contract Platform

2020 / 1.5 months / Product Design

Mastercard’s Smart Contract Platform (SCP) is a blockchain based solution for merchants to automize payments & services for their businesses. This product in particular, helps merchants create and manage smart contracts, and oversee related transactions for micro-payments, subscription, and other usecases.

I led product discussions from end to end, bringing together business, tech and product team to a common vision and conducted design iterations based on that.   Within 1.5 month, from wireframe to final prototype, this demo went through internal and external validations with high appraisal and successfully launched in early 2021.

The biggest design challenge is to distinguish 3 abstract concepts- templates, contracts, and agreements for merchants who are laymen to these terms. I used visual metaphors and archetypes borrowed from other domain, and applied subtly different page layouts as well as interactivity to suggest their differences. Follow-up tests affirmed merchants are able to grasp these concepts without prior knowledge.
